Sales Dataset

In the Big Mart Sales Dataset we have more than 10,000 rows and 12 coloumns.

In 12 coloums we have:

1. Item_Id (Each item have unique Id and we have 1559 Item in the Big Mart Data) Example:"FDA15","DRC01","FDN15","FDX07".

2. Item_Weight (Each item have Specified weight which we find with the help of Item_id).

3. In the third column we have Item_Fat_Content in which two category will be there 1.Low Fat and 2. Regular.

4. In the data fourth column have Item_Presence in the Big Mart.

5. Next column have the Item_type Example: Dairy, Soft Drinks.

6. In the next column we have Item_MRP.

7. Outlet_Identifier will be the next column in which We have 10 different Outlet in data Example: OUT049, OUT018.

8. From the next column we know about the Establishment of the Outlet.

9. In the 9 column we know about the Oulet Size that the Outlet Size is Low, Medium or High.

10. In the Next column we know about the Outlet location that in which tier of city Outlet is present.

11. Next column is of the Outlet_type that the Outlet is Supermarket ,Grocery Store etc.

12. In Last column we know about the Item Outlet Sales in which we get the information about how much Item will be sell in a particular Outlet.

This Prediction will be done by Xgboost Class of Machine Learning.
